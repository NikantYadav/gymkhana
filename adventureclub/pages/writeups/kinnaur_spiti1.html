Travelogue : Kinnaur and Spiti

This was the first High altitude mountain cycling expedition organized by Adventure Sports Club, IIT Kanpur.

Mountain Cycling is gaining popularity in India, but high altitude expeditions still remain a challenge for the most. A group of 11 people were selected for this expedition to Spiti Valley in Himachal Pradesh after a rigorous conditioning camp. Conditioning involved regular physical exercise at main stadium everyday in the morning. Timed cycling with load on bikes for stamina building, power cycling for testing limits and cycle repairing sessions at SAC.
